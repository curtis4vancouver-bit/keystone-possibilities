<!-- CONTEXT: Identity / 6 · YMYL Compliance Requirements -->
## 6 · YMYL Compliance Requirements

> **⚠️ CRITICAL — Health content is YMYL (Your Money or Your Life)**

All Protocol/Recomposition content MUST include:

<!-- CONTEXT: Identity / Mandatory Disclaimers -->
### Mandatory Disclaimers

1. **Video disclaimer** (spoken in every video):
   > "This is my personal experience and personal protocol. This is not medical advice. Always consult your healthcare provider before starting any new protocol."

2. **Description disclaimer** (in every video description):
   > "DISCLAIMER: The information in this video reflects my personal experience and is for educational purposes only. It is not intended as medical advice. Consult a qualified healthcare professional before making any changes to your health regimen."

3. **Blog disclaimer** (at top of every health article):
   > Block-quoted disclaimer with link to full medical disclaimer page.

<!-- CONTEXT: Identity / Content Rules -->
### Content Rules

- **Personal doses** are shared as "what I take" — never as recommendations
- **Published studies** must be linked when making mechanism claims
- **Side effects** must be disclosed honestly — no sugar-coating
- **Never diagnose** or prescribe in content
- **Age-gate** content where appropriate (YouTube settings)

## 7 · The Wolverine Stack — Series Bible

<!-- CONTEXT: Identity / Series Identity -->
### Series Identity

- **Name:** The Wolverine Stack
- **Concept:** A comprehensive performance and recovery protocol named for the relentless regeneration and resilience it's designed to deliver
- **Format:** Multi-episode deep dive with quarterly results updates
- **Thumbnail style:** Gold text, dark background, Wayne in power pose

<!-- CONTEXT: Identity / Episode Templates -->
### Episode Templates

**Deep Dive Episode:**
1. Hook (15 seconds) — dramatic result or statement
2. Compound introduction — what it is, dash-syllable pronunciation
3. Mechanism — how it works, simplified
4. Wayne's protocol — personal dosing and timing
5. Results — bloodwork, body comp, or performance data
6. Disclaimer — spoken, not just on-screen
7. CTA — subscribe, next episode tease

**Results Update Episode:**
1. Hook — "90 days on the Wolverine Stack. Here's what happened."
2. Before/after data — side-by-side bloodwork
3. Key wins — what improved
4. Honest challenges — what didn't work or had side effects
5. Protocol adjustments — what changed and why
6. Next 90 days — preview
7. Disclaimer and CTA
