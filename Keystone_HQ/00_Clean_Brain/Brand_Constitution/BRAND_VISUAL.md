## 1 · Universal Visual Rules

<!-- CONTEXT: Brand Visual / Image Generation Policy -->
### Image Generation Policy

> **⚠️ CRITICAL RULE — ALL [[AGENTS|AGENTS]]**
>
> **NEVER use the `generate_image` tool for any Keystone content.**
>
> Instead, always provide **Google Flow prompts** — structured text prompts that Wayne or the production pipeline feeds into Google's image generation tools manually.
>
> This ensures quality control, brand consistency, and Wayne's personal approval on all visual assets.

<!-- CONTEXT: Brand Visual / Typography Hierarchy -->
### Typography Hierarchy

| Level     | Usage                    | Style                          |
|-----------|--------------------------|--------------------------------|
| Display   | Thumbnails, hero banners | Bold, ALL CAPS, high contrast  |
| Heading   | Section titles, overlays | Semi-bold, title case          |
| Body      | Descriptions, blogs      | Regular weight, sentence case  |
| Caption   | Timestamps, credits      | Light weight, small size       |

<!-- CONTEXT: Brand Visual / Universal Rules -->
### Universal Rules

- **Aspect ratios:** 16:9 for YouTube, 9:16 for Shorts/Reels/TikTok, 1:1 for feed posts.
- **Safe zones:** Keep critical elements (text, faces) within 80% of the frame — platform UI overlays cut edges.
- **No stock photos.** All imagery is either Wayne's actual work/life or AI-generated via Google Flow with Wayne's approval.
- **No watermarks** on published content.
- **File naming:** `brand_platform_title_date` (e.g., `protocol_yt_wolverine-stack_20260528`).

<!-- CONTEXT: Brand Visual / 5 · Thumbnail Rules (All Brands) -->
## 5 · Thumbnail Rules (All Brands)

<!-- CONTEXT: Brand Visual / Non-Negotiable Rules -->
### Non-Negotiable Rules

1. **Bold typography.** If you can't read it on a phone screen, it's too small.
2. **High contrast.** Text must pop against the background — add drop shadows or overlays.
3. **Face visible** (Possibilities & Protocol). Emotion drives clicks. Wayne's expression must be readable.
4. **Maximum 4 words.** Thumbnails are billboards, not paragraphs.
5. **No clickbait imagery** that doesn't match the content.
6. **Test at 120×67 pixels.** That's how it looks in YouTube's sidebar — if it's illegible, redesign.

<!-- CONTEXT: Brand Visual / Thumbnail Checklist -->
### Thumbnail Checklist

- [ ] Text readable at mobile size?
- [ ] Brand palette colours used?
- [ ] Wayne's face visible and expressive? (N/A for Music)
- [ ] Under 4 words of text?
- [ ] High contrast between text and background?
- [ ] No clutter or competing elements?
- [ ] Correct aspect ratio (16:9)?

## 6 · [[Brand_Constitution/shared/AVATAR_RULES|Avatar Rules]]

> **⚠️ CRITICAL — ALL [[AGENTS|AGENTS]] MUST FOLLOW**

<!-- CONTEXT: Brand Visual / Wayne Stevenson's Avatar -->
### Wayne Stevenson's Avatar

When generating Google Flow prompts or describing Wayne for any visual content:

**ALWAYS say:**
> "My [[09_YouTube_Operations/Scripts_Approved/AVATAR-WAYNE|avatar Wayne]] Stevenson, reference to my picture"

**NEVER:**
- Describe Wayne's age, estimated age, or age range
- Describe specific physical traits (skin colour, hair colour, body type)
- Make assumptions about Wayne's appearance
- Use generic descriptors like "middle-aged man" or "older gentleman"

<!-- CONTEXT: Brand Visual / Why This Matters -->
### Why This Matters

Wayne's avatar is created from his actual reference photos. The AI image generation tools (via Google Flow) already have his likeness. Describing physical traits:
- Creates inconsistency across generated images
- Risks inaccurate or unflattering representations
- Wastes prompt space on information the model already has

<!-- CONTEXT: Brand Visual / Correct Google Flow Prompt Examples -->
### Correct Google Flow Prompt Examples

✅ **Correct:**
> "My [[09_YouTube_Operations/Scripts_Approved/AVATAR-WAYNE|avatar Wayne]] Stevenson, reference to my picture, standing on a construction site, wearing a hard hat, confident expression, professional lighting"

✅ **Correct:**
> "My [[09_YouTube_Operations/Scripts_Approved/AVATAR-WAYNE|avatar Wayne]] Stevenson, reference to my picture, power pose, dark gym background, dramatic side lighting, gold text overlay space on right"

❌ **Wrong:**
> "A 50-year-old muscular man with grey hair standing on a construction site"

❌ **Wrong:**
> "Middle-aged Caucasian male in athletic wear at a gym"

## 7 · Google Flow Prompt Structure

```
MOOD: [emotional tone]
COMPOSITION: [framing, rule of thirds, text overlay space]
STYLE: [photography style — cinematic, editorial, documentary]

```
