## 3 · Target Audience

<!-- CONTEXT: [[Brand_Constitution/protocol/IDENTITY|Identity]] / Primary: Sea-to-Sky Homeowners -->
### Primary: Sea-to-Sky Homeowners

- **Location:** Squamish, Whistler, Pemberton, Lions Bay, Britannia Beach
- **Situation:** Own property, considering additions, suites, or renovations
- **Pain points:**
  - Confused by Bill 44 and what it means for their lot
  - Don't know where to start with permits
  - Worried about costs and timelines
  - Unsure if their property qualifies for ADU development
  - Overwhelmed by building code requirements

<!-- CONTEXT: [[Brand_Constitution/protocol/IDENTITY|Identity]] / Secondary: Prospective Builders & Investors -->
### Secondary: Prospective Builders & Investors

- People considering purchasing property in the Sea-to-Sky for development
- Small-scale investors looking at ADU/suite rental income
- Other builders seeking code interpretation guidance
