## 🏛️ 1. Core Concept: Physics-Informed World Generation
Google's **Project Genie** and **Veo 3.1** allow you to translate standard architectural site plans, timber frame drawings, and terrain descriptions into interactive, photorealistic 3D environments. 

Instead of simple static CAD mockups, Genie models physical properties—gravity, water flow, steam wisps, wind speed through cedar trees, and sunlight angles. This allows you to create high-retention video content and high-ticket pitch decks showcasing the finished build before break-ground.
