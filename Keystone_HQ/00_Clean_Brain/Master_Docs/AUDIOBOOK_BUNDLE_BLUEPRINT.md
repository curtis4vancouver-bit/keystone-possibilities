## 1. Why High-Net-Worth Executives Pay $70 for an Audiobook
Affluent executives, founders, and builders do not want a padded, generic 15-hour audiobook filled with filler stories. They pay for **speed, density, and actionability**. 

To command **$70.00** instead of standard Audible pricing ($15.00), we package the asset as an **Audio-First Interactive System**:
- **Target Spoken Length:** **3 to 4 Hours** (~30,000 to 40,000 normalized words). This is the perfect length for a busy executive to consume over a single weekend or during a week of morning cardio blocks.
- **The "Builder's Framework":** The manuscript must treat physical body recomposition exactly like a construction project management problem:
  - **Slabs & Foundations:** Protein baselines & caloric structures.
  - **Change Orders:** Adjusting macros when fat loss stalls.
  - **Inspections & Audits:** Hormone blood panels and sleep metrics.

### Step 2: Establish the Spoken Normalization Table

* `T4` -> "T four"
* `HbA1c` -> "hemoglobin A one c"
* `ApoB` -> "apolipoprotein B"
* `SHBG` -> "sex hormone-binding globulin"
* `TRT` -> "testosterone replacement therapy"
