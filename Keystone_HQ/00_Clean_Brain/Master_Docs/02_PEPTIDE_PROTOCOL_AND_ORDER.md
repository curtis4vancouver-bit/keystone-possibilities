### 🌙 NIGHT PROTOCOL (Every Night Before Bed)
**"The Night Shift Shot"**
- **Goal:** Deep sleep and growth hormone pulse.
- **CJC-1295 (no DAC) / Ipamorelin:** 4 Units (200 mcg total — this is the Saturation Dose).
- **Schedule:** 5 Days On, 2 Days Off to prevent pituitary tachyphylaxis.
- **CRITICAL RULE:** Fasted for at least 2-3 hours prior. GLP-1s slow gastric emptying, so the final meal must be eaten 3 hours before. Insulin blunts the GH pulse.

**Foreman's Note on CJC Dosing:** 4 units (200 mcg) is the "Saturation Dose." In peptide science, the receptors for Growth Hormone Secretagogues generally "fill up" at around 100 mcg per compound. Taking 10 units wouldn't give you more muscle; it would just increase side effects (tingling hands, water retention/bloat). 4-5 units is the sweet spot for long-term growth and deep sleep.
