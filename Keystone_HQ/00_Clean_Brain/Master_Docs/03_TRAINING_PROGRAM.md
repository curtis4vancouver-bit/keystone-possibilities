## THE 5-DAY ROLLING ROTATION

Because you train 5 days a week (Mon-Fri) with weekends off for the Sunday cold plunge, the muscle groups roll through the week. Track the weekly shift below.

| Week | Monday | Tuesday | Wednesday | Thursday | Friday |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Chest & Back | Shoulders & Arms | Legs & Core | Chest & Back | Shoulders & Arms |
| 2 | Legs & Core | Chest & Back | Shoulders & Arms | Legs & Core | Chest & Back |
| 3 | Shoulders & Arms | Legs & Core | Chest & Back | Shoulders & Arms | Legs & Core |
| 4 | Chest & Back | Shoulders & Arms | Legs & Core | Chest & Back | Shoulders & Arms |

## WORKOUT A: CHEST & BACK (Foundation)
*25 Min Elliptical Post-Weights*

| Exercise | Sets | Reps | Note |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Superset:** Incline DB Press | 4 | 10-12 | Protect the spine; keep back flat. |
| **Superset:** Chest Supported Row | 4 | 10-12 | Pull with elbows; squeeze shoulder blades. |
| **Superset:** Flat Bench Press | 3 | 8-10 | Controlled descent. |
| **Superset:** Lat Pulldowns | 3 | 12-15 | Wide grip for "The V" frame. |
| **Superset:** Weighted Dips | 3 | To Failure | Lean forward for chest engagement. |
| **Superset:** Seated Cable Row | 3 | 12 | Focus on the lower lat stretch. |
| **Finisher:** DB Pullovers | 3 | 15 | Deep breath at the bottom to expand ribcage. |

## WORKOUT B: SHOULDERS & ARMS (Engine)
*25 Min Elliptical Post-Weights*

| Exercise | Sets | Reps | Note |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Primary:** Seated Military Press | 4 | 8-10 | Strictly seated to support the back. |
| **Secondary:** Lateral Raises | 4 | 15-20 | Pinkies up; keep the tension on the side delt. |
| **Superset:** Barbell Curls | 4 | 10-12 | No swinging; strictly biological tension. |
| **Superset:** Skull Crushers | 4 | 10-12 | Use an EZ bar if possible to save wrists. |
| **Superset:** Hammer Curls | 3 | 12 | Builds the "thickness" of the arm. |
| **Superset:** Rope Pushdowns | 3 | 15 | Flare the rope at the bottom. |
| **Finisher:** Wrist Curls | 3 | Failure | Forearm density for the builder's grip. |

## WORKOUT C: LEGS & CORE (Base)
*25 Min Elliptical Post-Weights*

| Exercise | Sets | Reps | Note |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Primary:** Leg Press | 4 | 15 | High feet on the platform to save knees. |
| **Secondary:** Goblet Squats | 3 | 12 | Keep the weight tight to the chest. |
| **Superset:** Leg Extensions | 3 | 15 | Slow negatives; 3 seconds down. |
| **Superset:** Seated Leg Curls | 3 | 15 | Squeeze the hamstrings hard. |
| **Calf:** Standing Calf Raises | 4 | 20 | Full stretch at the bottom. |
| **Core:** Dead Bugs | 3 | 15 | Slow and controlled; glue lower back to floor. |
| **Core:** Planks | 3 | 60 Sec | Tighten the glutes and core; no sagging. |
