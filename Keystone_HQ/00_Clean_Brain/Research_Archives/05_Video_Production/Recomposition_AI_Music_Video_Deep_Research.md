## VEO 3.1: THE FACE PROBLEM

### What Happens
- Upload your real face photo → **BLOCKED by safety classifiers**
- Google treats all real faces as potential deepfake violations
- Results in "Access Denied" or policy violation errors

### The Workaround: "Image Laundering"
1. Pass real photo through **Nano Banana 2** or **Midjourney**
2. Generate a stylized/illustrated "AI proxy" version of yourself
3. Feed the AI proxy into Veo 3.1's "Ingredients to Video"
4. Safety filters don't activate on the stylized version

### The Better Strategy: Hybrid Workflow
- **Veo 3.1** = environmental B-roll ONLY (waves, studio lights, atmospheric textures)
- **Kling 3.0** = character-specific DJ shots (no face restrictions, [[Brand_Constitution/protocol/IDENTITY|Identity]] Lock)
- **Composite in DaVinci Resolve** → seamless final output

## VISUAL CONSISTENCY: REFERENCE CHAINING

### The Problem
Random prompts = "AI lottery" — wildly different styles every generation.

### The Solution: Tail-to-Head Frame Continuity
1. Generate first clip using **album cover** as primary image prompt
2. Extract a **still from the final frame** of that clip
3. Feed that still as the **starting frame** for the next clip
4. Repeat across entire mix

This mathematically forces the AI to maintain lighting, color, and geometry.

### Prompt Wrapper (Use for EVERY Scene)
```
"Atmosphere: Deep teal and emerald color grading, volumetric god rays,
hyper-realistic, 35mm lens, high contrast, slow cinematic camera movement"
```
Append this fixed block to every scene description across the album's production.

### Final Step: Unified LUT in DaVinci Resolve
- AI clips naturally vary in contrast/saturation between generations
- Apply a single LUT or standardized color grade across ALL assembled clips
- This makes diverse AI clips feel like one intentionally directed film

## YOUTUBE COMPLIANCE FOR AI MUSIC VIDEOS

### AI Disclosure
- ✅ Check "Altered or synthetic content" toggle during upload
- ✅ This does NOT hurt algorithmic reach or monetization
- ❌ Failing to disclose = policy violation, strikes, forced labeling

### Content ID
- AI-generated music is NOT in the Content ID database (won't trigger claims)
- **Risk:** If AI hallucinates a copyrighted melody, Content ID WILL flag it
- **Fix:** YouTube now lets you replace claimed audio with royalty-free AI substitutes in Studio
- Pure AI content cannot be copyrighted (Supreme Court ruling)

### Bifurcated Algorithm (CRITICAL)
| YouTube Video Algorithm | YouTube Music Algorithm |
|---|---|
| Click-Through Rate (CTR) | Listen Completion Rate |
| Average View Duration (AVD) | Add-to-Library Rate |
| Session Duration | 72-Hour Replay Rate |

**The trap:** A cinematic visualizer may attract video viewers who click away before the song ends → YouTube Music algorithm interprets this as poor audio quality → penalizes discoverability on the streaming side.

**Fix:** Music must hook the listener independently of the visuals.

## OPTIMAL CONTENT FORMAT

### Duration Strategy
| Format | Length | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| **The Mix** | 30-60+ minutes | Maximum watch time, Smart TV lean-back, productivity companion |
| **Single Track** | 3-5 minutes | Radio edit, tight visual pacing at intro/drop/breakdown |
| **Shorts** | 15-60 seconds | Discovery engine → funnel viewers to long-form mixes |

### Smart TV = Your Audience
- YouTube serves **1 billion hours daily** to Smart TVs
- TV viewers are captive, lean-back audience
- Long-form deep house mixes = massive Session Duration signal
- 60-min video at 30% retention = 18 min watch time (outperforms short videos)

### Winning Visual Aesthetics (2026)

| Style | Description | Target Demographic |
|---|---|---|
| **Luxury Fashion / Louis Vuitton** | High-end boutiques, velvet textures, slow-motion runway, Parisian [[ARCHITECTURE|architecture]] | Premium workspace, designer showrooms |
| **Cinematic Nature / Sunset** | 4K tropical beaches, golden hour, coastal drone flights, ocean waves | Tropical Deep House, escapism/relaxation |
| **Night Drive / Cyberpunk** | Neon cityscapes, rain on windshields, urban canyons | Late-night focus, driving demographics |

### Technical Requirements
- **4K minimum** for Smart TV playback
- Slow, deliberate camera movements (NOT frenetic/strobe)
- Aggressive strobe/kinetic editing = kills watch time for ambient genres
- Must complement, not distract from, the primary use case (work/study/relax)
