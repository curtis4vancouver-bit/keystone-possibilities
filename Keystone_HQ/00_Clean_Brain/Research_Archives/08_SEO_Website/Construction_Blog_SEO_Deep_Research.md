## THE 5 RULES THAT MATTER MOST

### 1. Google March 2026 Core Update = "Total Reset"
- PBNs, expired domains, link farming, generic AI content → all DESTROYED
- Algorithm now maps your entire "digital footprint" — not just keywords
- Parasite SEO (renting subdirectories on news sites) = obsolete
- Content must prove verifiable human expertise or get suppressed

### 2. AI Overviews Steal 58% of Top-Result Clicks
- When AI Overview triggers, the #1 organic result loses 58% of its traffic
- Must optimize for AI citation, not just ranking position
- Requires: precise headings, schema markup, branded mentions across the web
- "Query fan-out" = AI breaks one search into dozens of sub-queries

### 3. IPTC Metadata Tag is MANDATORY for AI Images
- All AI-generated images MUST contain: `IPTC DigitalSourceType = TrainedAlgorithmicMedia`
- URI: `http://cv.iptc.org/newscodes/digitalsourcetype/trainedAlgorithmicMedia`
- Required across Google Search, Images, Maps, YouTube, Merchant Center
- WordPress 7.0 (April 2026) now reads IPTC fields natively
- **Tools for bulk tagging:** AI Image Tagger (Lightroom), PhotoTag.ai, Fairu.app, MetaVision

### 4. E-E-A-T is Non-Negotiable for Construction (YMYL)
| Dimension | What Google Demands | How Keystone Proves It |
|---|---|---|
| **Experience** | Firsthand physical involvement | Real job site photos, field error stories, project timelines |
| **Expertise** | Technical knowledge, credentials | BC Building Code deep-dives, structural explanations |
| **Authoritativeness** | Industry recognition | Local publication mentions, municipal citations |
| **Trustworthiness** | Transparency, accuracy | Clear AI labeling, real contact info, honest costs |

### 5. AI Images = NOT Copyrightable
- Pure AI-generated images have NO copyright protection
- Competitors can legally download and reuse your AI renders
- Copyright only attaches with substantial human post-production
- NEVER use AI for core brand assets that need trademark protection

## WHAT AI CAN AND CANNOT DO ON YOUR BLOG

### ✅ AI Allowed (Top of Funnel)
- Blog headers and thematic mood boards
- Conceptual "what-if" Bill 44 multiplex renders
- Virtual staging of empty completed builds
- Pre-construction visualization options for clients
- Material/facade variation studies

### ❌ AI Prohibited (Bottom of Funnel)
- Job site progress photos (legal liability)
- Completed portfolio projects (deceptive = E-E-A-T death)
- Team/personnel headshots (kills authenticity)
- Safety documentation (regulatory compliance)
- Anything presented as a real completed project

### The Optimal Blog Post Structure
1. **AI-generated header image** → captures attention, sets aesthetic theme
2. **Real unpolished job site photos** → in body text (framing, foundation, team on-site)
3. **AI concept renders** → for "what could be" visualization (clearly labeled)
4. **Real before/after photos** → from actual completed projects
5. Every AI image captioned: *"Concept visualization generated by artificial intelligence"*

## NORTH VANCOUVER CONTENT STRATEGY

### Bill 44 Multiplex Reality Check
- 60,000+ eligible lots in Metro Vancouver
- **Only 518 applications in 2025** — economic feasibility is narrow
- Build-to-hold economics are "fundamentally broken" (costs exceed $2.5M for full teardown)
- **Viable strategy:** Retain existing home + add secondary units (basement suite + laneway)
- Blog content demystifying these economics = high-intent lead magnet

### Net Zero & West Coast Modern
- Market shifted from "loud wealth" to high-performance subtle sophistication
- Warm woods, matte finishes, biophilic design
- Vancouver Zero Emissions Building Plan making Net Zero mainstream
- Requires: ventilated rain screens, passive solar orientation, airtight envelopes

## CANADIAN LEGAL FRAMEWORK

### Privacy
- No federal AI law (AIDA/Bill C-27 died before Parliament dissolved)
- Privacy Commissioners (federal + BC + AB + QC) actively enforcing existing frameworks
- Joint warning: no AI systems generating misleading imagery without consent
- Do NOT feed client floorplans or site data into public AI tools
- Subcontractor contracts must address AI data reuse

### Disclosure Best Practices
- 78% of organizations now mandate AI content transparency
- Use explicit captions: *"Concept visualization generated by artificial intelligence"*
- Labels in close proximity to image (not buried in footer)
- Consider standardized AI iconography alongside text

### Content Labeling Pipeline
1. Tag all existing AI-generated assets retroactively
2. Train team on disclosure workflows
3. Update vendor contracts to require AI disclosure
4. Implement bulk IPTC metadata processing before any CMS upload
