# PHONETIC BREAKDOWN RULES FOR GOOGLE FLOW VIDEO PRODUCTION


## MANDATORY FORMAT
All medical/scientific drug names and hard terms in video scripts MUST be broken down into simple lowercase hyphenated syllables for correct AI pronunciation in Google Flow.


## FORMAT: lowercase-hyphen-separated-syllables
- NO random capitalization or emphasis caps (WRONG: Re-TAT-ru-tide, Tir-ZEP-a-tide)
- ALL lowercase, simple hyphens between syllables (RIGHT: ret-a-tru-tide, tir-zep-a-tide)
- The purpose is phonetic pronunciation guidance for the AI voice, not visual emphasis

## RULES
1. Every hard medical/scientific term gets this treatment in ALL scripts
2. Format is always: simple lowercase syllables separated by hyphens
3. No caps emphasis anywhere in the phonetic version
4. Wayne confirmed the format explicitly as ret-a-tru-tide on 2026-06-10
5. This applies to ALL future content production scripts (LONG, SHORT, SCRIPT series)
6. Do NOT describe wardrobe in prompts — the avatar/character already has it built in
7. Only describe camera angles, background, and delivery style in VIDEO PROMPT sections
