## 2. CLINICAL PEPTIDE COMPARISON: SEMAGLUTIDE vs. TIRZEPATIDE vs. RETATRUTIDE

*   **Semaglutide (Single Agonist):** Targets GLP-1. Reliable, baseline weight loss protocol.
*   **Tirzepatide (Dual Agonist):** Targets GLP-1 and GIP. Highly effective; Wayne's current foundational tool.
*   **Retatrutide (Triple Agonist / "Triple G"):** Targets GLP-1, GIP, and Glucagon.
    *   **Efficacy:** TRIUMPH-1 Phase 3 trials showed up to 28% body weight loss (surgical-level efficacy).
    *   **Glucagon Mechanism:** Directly stimulates energy expenditure (increases metabolic resting rate/runs the body's "furnace" hotter). 
    *   **Unique Advantage:** Patients report higher relative appetite than on Tirzepatide, allowing them to eat enough to feed and maintain skeletal muscle while still losing fat.

## 3. THE STRUCTURAL RISK: MUSCLE LOSS ("STRUCTURAL COLLAPSE")
*   **The Threat:** Standard GLP-1 agonists can result in up to 35% of total weight lost coming from lean muscle mass. Losing muscle is equivalent to removing a building's load-bearing framing.
*   **Mitigation Strategy:** 
    *   Establish high-protein nutritional baselines.
    *   Implement consistent heavy resistance training.
    *   Utilize DXA scans to monitor precise body composition changes (ensuring fat loss, not structural tissue loss).
    *   Address the underlying foundation: lifestyle modification is mandatory to prevent the standard post-protocol weight regain (typically 2/3 of weight lost within one year of cessation).

## 4. FDA REGULATORY UPDATES & FUTURE TIMELINES (2026-2028)

### A. GLP-1 Compounding Ban
*   **Proposal:** FDA proposed ban on large-scale compounding of GLP-1/GIP medications.
*   **Key Date:** Public comment period closed June 29, 2026.
*   **Strategic Risk:** Banning compounding risks driving consumers to unverified online "research chemical" gray markets (analogous to building with uncertified steel lacking mill test reports).

### B. Pharmacy Compounding Advisory Committee (PCAC) Status
*   **Key Date:** July 23–24, 2026.
*   **Agenda:** Regulatory review of 7 major clinical peptides, including **BPC-157**, **TB-500**, and **MOTS-c**.
*   **Status:** Category 2 safety restrictions have been removed, but final approved compounding status remains in regulatory limbo.

### C. Retatrutide Commercial Timeline
*   **Phase 3 Trials:** Running through 2026.
*   **Earliest Market Approval:** Projected late 2027 or early 2028.
