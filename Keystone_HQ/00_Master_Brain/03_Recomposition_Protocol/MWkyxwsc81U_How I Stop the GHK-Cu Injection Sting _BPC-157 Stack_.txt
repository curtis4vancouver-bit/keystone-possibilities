# Peptide Protocol: Neutralizing GHK-Cu Injection Sting with BPC-157

## Issue
- GHK-Cu (Copper Peptide) injections cause an intense, painful stinging sensation ("battery acid") at the injection site.

## Solution / Strategy
- **Stacking Protocol**: Draw **BPC-157** into the same syringe as the **GHK-Cu** prior to injection.
- **Mechanism**: BPC-157 buffers the acidity of the GHK-Cu and promotes immediate localized tissue healing, eliminating the injection pain entirely.
