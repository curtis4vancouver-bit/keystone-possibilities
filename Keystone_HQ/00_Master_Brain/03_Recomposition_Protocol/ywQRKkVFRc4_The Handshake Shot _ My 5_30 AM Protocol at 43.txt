# The Handshake Shot: 5:30 AM Protocol (Age 43)

## Overview
A highly disciplined daily morning protocol designed for rapid tissue repair and systemic inflammation mitigation.

## The Protocol
- **Timing**: 5:30 AM daily (Alarm set for 5:15 AM).
- **State**: Fasted (prior to consuming any food or coffee).
- **Duration**: 30-day continuous cycle.
- **Compounds**: Dual-compound stack (the "Handshake Shot")
  - **Compound 1**: Targets tissue repair.
  - **Compound 2**: Targets inflammation reduction.
- **Logging**: Rigorous daily entries modeled after a professional job-site inspection report.

## Results & Progression
- **Sleep Improvement**: By Day 4 of the cycle, achieved 6.5 hours of uninterrupted sleep.
- **Pain Resolution**: Alleviated chronic shoulder pain that had caused wakefulness at 3:00 AM for the preceding 2 years.
- **Outcome**: Measurable structural recovery achieved early in the 30-day window.
