## The Builder's Protocol: 4 Pillars of Structural Preservation
To prevent muscle wasting and tissue degradation during aggressive GLP-1/Triple G agonist protocols, the following shoring protocols must be deployed:

### Pillar 1: High Protein Shoring
* **Target**: 200g of protein daily (non-negotiable).
* **Sources**: Whey isolate, ground beef, whole eggs.
* **Timing**: Feedings every 3 hours to maintain muscle protein synthesis.

### Pillar 2: Heavy Eccentric Resistance Training
* **Modality**: Heavy compound barbell lifts (Squats, Deadlifts, Overhead Press).
* **Mechanism**: Loading muscles under heavy tension sends an anti-catabolic signal to retain tissue. Avoid over-reliance on cardio/Peloton.
