# The Builder's Protocol: 4 Pillars to Preserve Muscle on GLP-1s

During GLP-1 therapy (Ozempic, Mounjaro, Retatrutide, etc.), approximately 35% of weight lost can come from muscle rather than fat. To protect structural integrity during this phase, implement the following four-pillar protocol:

### The 4 Pillars
1. **High Protein Intake:** Consume 200g of protein daily to preserve lean tissue.
2. **Heavy Resistance Training:** Focus on heavy barbell compound lifts to signal muscle retention.
3. **Tissue Repair Peptides:** Administer BPC-157 and TB-500 to support ongoing recovery and joint/tissue repair.
4. **Collagen & Skin Support:** Use GHK-Cu (Copper Peptide) to prevent skin laxity ("Ozempic face") and support systemic collagen synthesis.

*Note: This protocol remains identical regardless of the specific GLP-1 agonist used (Ozempic, Mounjaro, or Retatrutide).*
