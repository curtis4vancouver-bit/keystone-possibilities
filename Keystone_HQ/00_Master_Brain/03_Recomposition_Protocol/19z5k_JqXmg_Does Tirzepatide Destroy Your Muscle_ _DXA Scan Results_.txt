Does Tirzepatide Destroy Your Muscle? (DXA Scan Results)

Contrary to the common belief that tirzepatide causes excessive muscle loss, clinical data and scans reveal a different story:

1. Fat Loss vs. Lean Mass Ratio (SURMOUNT Trial):
   - DXA scan data shows approximately 75% fat loss versus only 20% lean mass loss (a highly favorable 3:1 ratio).

2. Intramuscular Fat Clearance:
   - MRI scans demonstrate that tirzepatide clears fat stored inside the muscles.
   - As a result, the muscle tissue you retain is of significantly higher quality and density.

Key Takeaway:
To optimize the retention of high-quality lean mass while on a tirzepatide protocol, ensure you train hard and maintain resistance progression.
