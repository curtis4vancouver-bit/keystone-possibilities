# Protocol Warning: Retatrutide & GLP-1 Muscle Wasting Risks

## Summary of Case Study
* **Timeline**: 6 months on Retatrutide.
* **Results**: 25 lbs total weight loss.
* **DEXA Metrics**: 10 lbs of lost weight was pure muscle mass (40% total muscle wasting).

## Key Takeaways & Risks
* **Structural Compromise**: Celebrating scale weight reduction without protecting lean body mass results in a structurally compromised system ("building on a rotting frame").
* **Rebar Analogy**: Rapid muscle loss is structurally equivalent to ripping 40% of the rebar out of a concrete foundation. The exterior looks acceptable, but the interior integrity is dangerously compromised.
* **Cardiovascular Impact**: Muscle catabolism is systemic and affects cardiac muscle tissue ("your heart is fragile").

## Actionable Recomposition Strategy
1. **Monitor Lean Mass**: Do not rely on scale weight alone. Utilize regular DEXA scans to track fat-to-muscle loss ratios.
2. **Protect Structure**: Ensure adequate protein intake and resistance training protocols are maintained throughout peptide cycles to prevent severe catabolic muscle wasting.
