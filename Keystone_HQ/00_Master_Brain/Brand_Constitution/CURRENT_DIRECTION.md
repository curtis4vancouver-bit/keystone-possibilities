## 2 · Protocol / Recomposition — Current Direction

<!-- CONTEXT: Current Direction / Primary Focus: Wolverine Stack Series (Flagship) -->
### Primary Focus: Wolverine Stack Series (Flagship)

**Goal:** Establish the Wolverine Stack as the signature Protocol series — a comprehensive peptide/performance protocol documented with real bloodwork and results.

#### Target Audience

- Men 35-55 interested in performance optimization
- People researching peptides, TRT, and longevity protocols
- Viewers who want real results, not just theory
- Health-conscious individuals seeking science-backed protocols

#### Content Strategy: Real Results Content

- **Every video must include proof.** Bloodwork, body composition, measurable outcomes.
- **The Wolverine Stack** is the flagship series — all other content supports or branches from it.
- **Gold thumbnails** are mandatory — the 40% CTR improvement is proven.
- **Dash-syllable pronunciation** for all pharmaceutical/peptide names in scripts.

#### Active Content Priorities

1. **Wolverine Stack Series** — ongoing multi-part deep dive
   - Episode structure: What → Why → How → Results
   - Each episode focuses on one compound or one measurable outcome
2. **90-Day Bloodwork Updates** — quarterly check-ins with real data
3. **Compound Deep Dives** — individual breakdowns (Sem-a-glue-tide, BPC-157, etc.)
4. **Protocol Stacking Logic** — how and why compounds are combined
5. **Recovery & Sleep Optimization** — supporting content for the core stack

#### Visual Direction

- **Gold typography on dark backgrounds** — every thumbnail, every graphic
- **Wayne's avatar in power poses** — confident, strong, results-driven
- **Bloodwork data visualizations** — make the numbers dramatic and readable
- **Dark, moody lighting** — premium feel, not clinical

#### Platform Priority

1. **YouTube** (primary — long-form authority)
2. **Instagram** (Reels + carousel posts with data)
3. **TikTok** (short-form hooks driving to YouTube)
4. **Website** (blog mirrors for SEO + affiliate links)
