The "Big" element requires the script to promise a significant, highly desirable physiological result from the outset. The "Easy" component dictates that the upcoming information must be presented as an accessible protocol or a step-by-step heuristic, mitigating the viewer's fear of overwhelming complexity. Even when discussing intricate metabolic pathways, the hook must assure the viewer that the ultimate takeaway is actionable. The "New" factor ensures the data feels novel.
