## HEALTH CHANNEL ADVERTISING RESTRICTIONS

### Meta Health Ad Rules (CRITICAL)
- ❌ NO second-person health claims: "Are you suffering from..." "Do you struggle with..."
- ❌ NO before-and-after imagery (including IMPLIED transformations)
- ❌ NO showing product next to idealized fit person
- ✅ MUST include: "This product is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease"
- ✅ Use third-person, product-first, mechanism-focused language

### Google Health Ad Rules
- ❌ Cannot use health data for personalized targeting
- ❌ Cannot promote experimental treatments or clinical trial recruitment
- ❌ No protected health information (PHI) in tracking/analytics
- ✅ Must use server-side tagging, disable Google Signals
- ✅ Geo-target to approved markets only

### Canada-Specific (Health Canada / PAAB)
- Health product ads must not be false, misleading, or deceptive
- Claims related to treatment outcomes face intense scrutiny
- Ads directed to healthcare professionals require PAAB pre-clearance

**Major BPCPA amendments take effect August 1, 2026:**
- Restricts mandatory private arbitration clauses
- New rules for "future performance contracts" (threshold: $50 cumulative)
- Expanded regulated goods: heat pumps, solar PV added alongside HVAC

## CONSTRUCTION AD STRATEGY — REVISED

### What TO Do
- Use **real job site footage** as primary creative (time-lapses, walkthroughs)
- Use AI for **editing, pacing, resizing** across placements
- Use AI for **conceptual visualization** of potential lot transformations
- Include customer testimonials (real people, real projects)
- Display BC license number, WCB coverage, insurance details

### What NOT To Do
- ❌ Do NOT fabricate portfolio projects with AI
- ❌ Do NOT use AI-generated video of yourself in Google Ads
- ❌ Do NOT upload proprietary pricing/plans into AI tools
- ❌ Do NOT imply AI renders are completed projects

### Recommended Disclosure Language
- Conceptual renders: *"This video utilizes AI visualization to demonstrate potential lot transformations. Not an actual completed project."*
- Enhanced real footage: *"Real project footage. Pacing and editing assisted by AI technologies."*
- General: *"In this ad, we used AI to help visualize [concept]. All designs verified by our licensed project management team."*

## AD SPECS BY PLACEMENT

| Placement | Ratio | Length | Viewer Behavior |
|---|---|---|---|
| YouTube Shorts / Reels | 9:16 vertical | 15-30s | Fast scroll, immediate hook |
| Connected TV (CTV) | 16:9 horizontal | 30-60s | Lean-back, 95% completion |
| Standard In-Stream | 16:9 horizontal | 15s | Must deliver value before 5s skip |
| Bumper Ads | 16:9 or 1:1 | 6s | Non-skippable awareness |

### CTA Best Practices
- Place CTA early (not just at end — most viewers won't see it)
- High-consideration services: use low-friction CTAs
  - ✅ "View Our Local Projects" / "Read the Case Study"
  - ❌ "Hire Us Today" / "Book Now"
- Disclosure: minimum 12pt font, on-screen for 3+ seconds
