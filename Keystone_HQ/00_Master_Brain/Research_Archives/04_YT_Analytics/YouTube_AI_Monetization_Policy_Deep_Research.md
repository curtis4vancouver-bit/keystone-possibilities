### What Triggers Disclosure

## "INAUTHENTIC CONTENT" POLICY (July 2025)

### This is a CHANNEL-LEVEL penalty, not per-video
If reviewers flag your channel, the ENTIRE channel loses monetization.

### What Gets Flagged
- ❌ Identical video structures across uploads (template clones)
- ❌ Generic synthesized VO with unrelated background footage
- ❌ Reading scraped text over random imagery
- ❌ High-volume posting with no variation in pacing/style
- ❌ No unique narrative, personal insight, or transformative value

### What Keeps You Safe
- ✅ Highly specific Veo 3.1 prompts matching narration concepts
- ✅ Original scripts with personal anecdotes and expert synthesis
- ✅ Dynamic editing: text overlays, zooms, transitions, precise A/V sync
- ✅ Each video feels editorially distinct (not templated)

### The Standard: "Human-in-the-Loop"
> There is NO mathematical ratio (e.g., 20% human / 80% AI) that guarantees safety.
> Enforcement is **qualitative** — focusing on synthesis of IDEAS, not volume of synthetic pixels.

## ADVERTISER CPM IMPACT

### The Reality
Some premium advertisers opt OUT of synthetic content placements.

- Google Ads "Content Suitability" settings let brands exclude AI-labeled content
- Risk-averse brands default to most restrictive settings
- Result: **smaller pool of bidding advertisers = potentially lower CPM**

### What This Means
- Your pipeline is efficient for **scaling production volume**
- But each view may yield **slightly lower revenue** than traditional filmed content
- The 20s human intro helps mitigate this (advertisers see real human = less risk)
- Factor lower CPM into financial models
