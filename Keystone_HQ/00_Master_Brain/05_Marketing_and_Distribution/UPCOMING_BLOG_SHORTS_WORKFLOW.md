# UPCOMING MILESTONE: The Blog & Shorts Distribution Workflow

*This document serves as the pinned reference for the upcoming pipeline expansion. This workflow bridges the gap between the Google Flow video creation and the final SEO/traffic distribution across the web.*

---

## 1. The High-End SEO Blog Protocol
Once the Google Flow video is created and rendered, the script must be converted into a highly optimized, high-end digital blog format that ranks fast.

**Mandatory Blog Requirements:**
- **Video Up Top**: The finalized YouTube video MUST be embedded at the very top of the blog to immediately capture traffic.
- **Brave Search Verification**: All data within the blog must be double-checked and verified via Brave Search.
- **Authority Looping**: Must include properly formatted links to all Keystone authority sites and previous related articles.
- **Alt-Tag Optimization**: Any photos or B-roll images inserted into the blog must have properly descriptive alt-tags for SEO indexing.
- **Snippets**: Schema markup and rich snippets must be properly formatted and inserted.
- **Dual-Subscribe CTAs**:
  1. A clear subscribe button/link at the bottom directing to the **Protocols YouTube Channel**.
  2. A clear subscribe button/link directing to the **Keystone Recomposition Music/Studio**.

---

## 2. The Shorts Traffic Loop
To drive immediate traffic to the newly published blog and long-form video, a series of YouTube Shorts must be created directly referencing the main content.

**Mandatory Shorts Requirements:**
- **Content Origin**: Snipped and referenced directly from the main 8-minute video/script.
- **Timing**: Must be uploaded *immediately after* the blog goes live to drive traffic into the loop.
- **Omni-Channel Distribution**: These shorts must be pushed to:
  1. YouTube Shorts
  2. Facebook Reels
  3. Instagram Reels
  4. TikTok
