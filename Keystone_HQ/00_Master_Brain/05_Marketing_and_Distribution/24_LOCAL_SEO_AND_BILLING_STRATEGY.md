## 2. Google Cloud Platform (GCP) Credit & API Billing Mechanics

To manage background local SEO audits and SDR lead generation pipelines efficiently, align with the following Google billing parameters:

### A. The $300 Welcome Credit
* **Terms**: GCP provides a **$300 welcome credit** valid for 90 days to new accounts for testing services (like Vertex AI or Maps API).
* **Verification**: Requires linking a valid Credit/Debit card during sign-up. Google runs a temporary $1 authorization hold to verify identity.
* **Auto-Billing Protection**: Google guarantees that **cards are not automatically charged during the free trial**. They do not start billing when the trial ends unless the user explicitly upgrades the billing account manually in the console.

### B. Multiple Account Credit Farming (The Limits)
* **Creation**: New Google Accounts can be created to access the trial.
* **The Constraint**: Google utilizes device fingerprinting, browser cookies, and card-hash tracking. Reusing the **same credit card** across 3 to 5 different Google Accounts to continuously farm $300 credits will trigger Google's fraud prevention system, leading to immediate suspension of all associated billing accounts.
* **Matching Spend**: There is no "match spend" requirement on the base trial; it is a direct credit.

### C. Google AI Studio's Free Tier
* **Alternative**: You **do not need a billing account or a credit card** if you generate your API keys directly through **Google AI Studio** rather than GCP / Vertex AI.
* **Limits**: AI Studio provides a **100% Free Tier** for Gemini 2.0 / 1.5 Flash and Pro (allowing up to 15 requests per minute and 1 million tokens per minute), which is sufficient for automated SEO audits, draft cold emails, and calculations.
