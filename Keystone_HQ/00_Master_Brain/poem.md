# Voice of the Builder

Between the static hum of screens,
A voice cuts through — direct, alive.
No keys, no clicks, no in-betweens,
Just words that make the code arrive.

He speaks and mountains move to plan,
Steel bends to vision, pixels yield.
From Sea-to-Sky, the builder's hand
Now reaches through a sonic field.

The future isn't typed. It's spoken.
The bridge between the mind and machine
Is not a wire. It's not a token.
It's Wayne — commanding the unseen.
