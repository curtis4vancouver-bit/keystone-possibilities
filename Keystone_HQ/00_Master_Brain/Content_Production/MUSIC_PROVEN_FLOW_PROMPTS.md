# Proven Google Flow Video Prompts — Ana Stevenson DJ Set
> Saved: 2026-06-09 | Status: PROVEN WORKING | Character: Ana Stevenson

## Style Notes
- **Setting:** Dark stage, black background, minimal set
- **Lighting:** Dramatic rim light, single spotlight, warm tones
- **Atmosphere:** Smoke/haze drifting behind or around legs
- **Vibe:** Confident, sexy, dancing while playing controls
- **NO:** Headsets, headphones, extra props, crowd, extra people
- **ENDING:** Every clip must end with a hard digital glitch cutting to black in the final second

---

## Prompt 1 — Wide Dancing Shot
> A stunning woman with long dark hair stands behind a DJ controller on a dark stage. Soft warm smoke drifts behind her. She sways her hips and moves to the beat, one hand on the controller knob, the other raised. Black background, dramatic rim lighting. She looks confident and sexy. In the final second the image glitches hard with digital distortion artifacts and cuts to pure black.

## Prompt 2 — Hands Close-Up to Zoom Out
> Extreme close-up of feminine hands with dark nails turning knobs and sliding faders on a DJ controller. Moody low-key lighting, shallow depth of field. The camera slowly pulls back to reveal her full silhouette dancing behind the decks, backlit with a single warm spotlight and wisps of haze. In the final second the footage glitches with scan lines and static, cutting sharply to black.

## Prompt 3 — Low Angle Hero Shot
> A beautiful woman DJ seen from a low angle, standing behind her controller on a dark stage. She rolls her shoulders and bobs her head to the music, fingers gliding across the mixer. Smoke curls around her legs. A single overhead spotlight casts sharp shadows across her face and body. She smirks at the camera. In the final second the image fractures with a hard digital glitch and snaps to black.

## Template Pattern
When generating NEW prompts in this style, follow this structure:
1. **Subject** — Describe Ana's position and what she's doing (dancing, playing controls)
2. **Setting** — Dark stage, black background, DJ controller
3. **Atmosphere** — Smoke, haze, minimal but moody
4. **Lighting** — Single spotlight, rim light, warm tones, dramatic shadows
5. **Mood** — Confident, sexy, effortless cool
6. **Ending** — ALWAYS end with: "In the final second the image/footage glitches with [specific glitch type] and cuts/snaps to black."

### Glitch Variations to Rotate:
- "digital distortion artifacts"
- "scan lines and static"
- "hard digital glitch"
- "RGB split and data corruption"
- "pixelated fracture lines"
- "VHS tracking errors"
