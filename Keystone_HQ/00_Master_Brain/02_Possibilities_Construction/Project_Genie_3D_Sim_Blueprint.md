## 🏛️ 1. Core Concept: Physics-Informed World Generation

Google's **Project Genie** and **Veo 3.1** translate architectural site plans, timber frame drawings, and terrain descriptions into interactive, photorealistic 3D environments. 

Instead of simple static CAD mockups, Genie models physical properties—gravity, water flow, steam wisps, wind speed through cedar trees, and sunlight angles. This creates high-retention video content and high-ticket pitch decks showcasing the finished build before breaking ground.
