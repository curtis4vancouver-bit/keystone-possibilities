## Uncovering AI Crawler Preferences

I discovered that AI search crawlers increasingly rely on technical signals; for instance, integrating comparison tables and utilizing the newly popularized 'llm.txt' standard can boost AI citation coverage by over 30%. ## Optimizing Content Freshness and Citations
My analysis reveals a high rate of 'citation decay' in conversational search, with over half of AI-cited sources being less than three months old. To resolve this, content strategies must transition to a 'citation-first' structure—positioning direct, fact-dense answers within the first 60 words of a page—and implement a quarterly schedule to refresh key statistics and data points. This ensures the brand remains on the critical 'Day One List' that conversational engines compile during early buyer research phases.
